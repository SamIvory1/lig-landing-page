Welcome to Lost in Groove

Allow me to introduce myself, I am George Heaton the founder of Lost in Groove LTD. I have a passion for house music, from melodic to techno and everything in between. This brand is founded on that love for all things house.

Lost in Groove aims create a brand that offers people the opportunity to express their love for all things house through high quality, subtle, stylish, and timeless clothing that can be worn for any occasion. It should be an extension of yourselves and allow you to characterise your passion.
